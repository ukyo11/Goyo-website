Company History
Zhicheng Semiconductor History

Growth trajectory leading China's CVD SiC localization since founding in 2017.

2017 · 12
Zhicheng Semiconductor founded. R&D of CVD SiC coated graphite components initiated.
2018
Dongguan factory established. In-house SiC coated graphite Susceptor successfully developed. First customer validation passed — import substitution achieved.
2019 ~ 2020
ISO certified. High-tech enterprise certification. Guangzhou factory established (Nov 2020). SiC coating service launched → solar market entry. Bulk SiC product R&D initiated.
2021 ~ 2022
Dongguan factory 2nd expansion completed. Bulk SiC & sintered SiC prototyping begins. Mature product revenue surges. Ranked #8 globally, #1 in China in CVD SiC components (QY Research 2022).
2023
Monthly shipment capacity 8,000+ established. Designated national-level "Little Giant" enterprise. Annual revenue ¥480M CNY. New Dongguan production base in preparation.
